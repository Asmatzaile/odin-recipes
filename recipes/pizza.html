Pizza

Description

A pizza. Perfect for parties or for when you don't feel like cooking - not even an egg.

Ingredients

  • A frozen pizza

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 220 degrees Celsius.
  2. When it's hot, put the pizza on a rack in the oven.
  3. Wait 10-12 minutes and check if it's done. If it is, carefully remove the pizza. If it isn't, check again in two minutes and repeat.
