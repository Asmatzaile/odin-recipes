Omelette

Description

It gives you warmth. It gives you love. Everyone praise the mighty omelette.

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • A spoonful of olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and beat them.
  2. Add the salt, the pepper and the herbs.
  3. Heat a pan and put the oil
  4. Fry the eggs for twenty seconds, turn around, keep ten seconds
